The first burger draft was rejected because its ingredients floated apart; it is not included in the application.

Add-on transparency was prepared with a local, one-off chroma-key preprocessing step using Pillow. That build-time tooling is not part of the app. Final PNG SHA-256 checksums are recorded in `ASSET_CHECKSUMS.md`.

The six `*_mask.png` resources are deterministic merchant-style author masks derived from the approved base photographs and generated by `Tools/GenerateAuthorMasks.swift`. The masks resolve touches and localize the reconstruction animation. The finished state is a complete reviewed destination photograph so the prototype cannot expose seams from a partial composite. They contain no third-party content and can be regenerated locally.
